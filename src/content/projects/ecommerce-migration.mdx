## Problem / Context
The client was struggling with a decade-old custom monolith platform that couldn't handle Black Friday traffic spikes and was notoriously difficult to update or maintain.

## What I Built
We orchestrated a full migration to a headless Shopify architecture. We used Remix run to build an incredibly fast custom storefront that interacts with Shopify's Storefront API.

## Architecture / Implementation
- **Frontend**: Remix (React), styled-components.
- **Backend**: Node.js microservices for custom fulfillment workflows using Prisma for a sidecar database.
- **Platform**: Shopify Plus (Storefront API).
- **Deployment**: Vercel for the frontend, Heroku for worker processes.

## Challenges & Solutions
Migrating tens of thousands of customer accounts and their hashed passwords over to Shopify safely. We built a custom multi-pass authentication flow that allowed users to keep their old passwords without realizing the underlying platform had changed.

## Outcomes & Lessons Learned
The new website handled the subsequent Black Friday with absolutely zero downtime, resulting in a 40% year-over-year increase in conversion rate due to significantly improved page load speeds.
